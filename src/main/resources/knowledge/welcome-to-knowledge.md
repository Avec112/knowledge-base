# Welcome to KnowledgeBase

This space is organized in two levels:

1. **Category**
2. **Article**

Use the menu on the left to browse by category and open articles.

## Navigation and Search

- Expand or collapse categories in the tree menu
- Use search above the menu to quickly jump to a category or article
- Use the small toggle button under search to expand/collapse all categories

## Category Ordering

Categories can be reordered by drag and drop:

1. Drag a category up or down in the menu
2. Drop it where you want it
3. The new order is saved and kept after restart

## Article Status

Articles support two statuses:

- `DRAFT` - work in progress, visible to admins in editing context
- `PUBLISHED` - ready for normal readers

When creating or editing an article, choose the status in the form.

## For Administrators

### Categories

- **Create**, **Edit**, and **Delete** categories from the left button group
- You can also create a category directly from the article form by selecting **Create** in the Category field

### Articles

- **Create**, **Edit**, **Preview**, **Save**, and **Delete** from the right button group
- Write content in Markdown
